About The Institution____


Good education encompasses instilling moral, aesthetic, athletic and intellectual values in the citizens of tomorrow. GNIT Nagpur established in 2009 by GNES society is one of the top engineering colleges under RTMNU is approved by All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi and Director of Technical Education, Maharashtra and NAAC Accredited. The college has lush green surrounding which provides conducive environment for students to grow not only as good Engineers but as world class citizens too. The campus is situated in the vicinity of MIDC Kalmeshwar which is the heart of the industrial zone. Spread over a sprawling 25 acres, the natural form of the landscape has been preserved.

The college has developed the state of-the-art infrastructure, well-equipped laboratories, modern library, state of-the-art computer center, classrooms equipped with modern teaching aids and multimedia projection facilities, hygienic cafeteria, sufficient parking space, modern facilities for both indoor and outdoor games, playground and well equipped gymkhana etc. These facilities create conducive environment for teaching-learning process. The interiors have been crafted keeping in mind the fact that ‘ambience does inspire and stimulate intellectual endeavors‘.

We offer best quality technical education to produced skilled technical manpower meeting international standards and global requirements ensuring optimal utilization of available resources.We are committed to nurture the required expertise under one roof by creating competent and motivated technocrats and managers of tomorrow, who will use their technical knowledge and managerial qualities to confidently and successfully lead the organization. For the overall development of the technocrats GNIT provided the best nurtured environment to the student by providing best facilities of hostel, transportation, cafeteria, soft skill training, book bank scheme and various curricular & extra curricular activities for the staff and students. Integrated teaching methodology is used for the enhancement of the students. Guest lectures from industry professionals and renowned academicians and industrial training tour are arranged to learn professional ethics.

Progress is the activity of today and the assurance of tomorrow. Professional society activities, Rotract club and Entrepreneurship development cell for student’s guides them to inbuilt professional ethical environment and relevant exposure towards industry and R&D organization. Interacting with industries, alumni, and society by offering mutually beneficial services, creating unshakable trust and maintaining high morals is the real motif of the organization. The students coming out of this institution are absorbed in several industries, education institutes, multi-national companies in India and abroad.

We invite you to come and be a part of this exciting journey, excitement that stems from insight, understanding and fulfillment of one‘s true potential and most importantly from succeeding while helping other succeed, because ”Coming together is a beginning, keeping together is progress and working together is success”.
