Education Society

To become a world class, globally competitive and flexible technical and management institution, responsive to the growth of an individual, society, and the institute itself satisfying the developmental needs of the people of Maharashtra and India. To serve as a centre for fostering the co-operation and the exchange of ideas between the academicians and the research community on one hand and welcome the industrial community and the Government organizations to promote the entrepreneurship development among the students.
